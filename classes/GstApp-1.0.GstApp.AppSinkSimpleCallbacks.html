    GstApp-1.0 ▶ GstApp ▶ AppSinkSimpleCallbacksSince 1.28

    A set of callbacks that can be installed on the appsink with gst_app_sink_set_simple_callbacks().

    Unlike GstAppSinkCallbacks this can also be used from bindings.

    Enumeration (GI Enum)

    Graphene-1.0 ▶ Graphene ▶ EulerOrderSince 1.2

    Specify the order of the rotations on each axis.

    The Graphene.EulerOrder.DEFAULT value is special, and is used as an alias for one of the other orders.
